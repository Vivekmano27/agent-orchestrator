# Competitor Analysis Skill

Research and document the competitive landscape for any product category.

## Analysis Template

```markdown
# Competitive Analysis: [Product Category]

## Market Overview
[2-3 sentences about the market size, trends, and dynamics]

## Competitor Matrix
| Feature | Our Product | [Competitor 1] | [Competitor 2] | [Competitor 3] |
|---------|------------|----------------|----------------|----------------|
| [Feature 1] | ✅/❌/🔄 | ✅/❌/🔄 | ✅/❌/🔄 | ✅/❌/🔄 |
| Pricing | [price] | [price] | [price] | [price] |
| Target User | [who] | [who] | [who] | [who] |

## Per-Competitor Deep Dive
### [Competitor Name]
- **Strengths:** [what they do well]
- **Weaknesses:** [where they fall short]
- **Pricing:** [model and tiers]
- **Market Position:** [premium/mid/budget]
- **Key Differentiator:** [unique selling point]

## Our Differentiation
- [What we do that no competitor does]
- [Underserved segment we can capture]

## Recommendations
1. [Strategic recommendation based on analysis]
```

## Process
1. Identify top 3-5 competitors (search web if available)
2. Map feature overlap and gaps
3. Analyze pricing models
4. Identify underserved segments
5. Recommend differentiation strategy
